Design choice

Why one active project?

Agents made starting and parallel execution dramatically cheaper. They did not make product judgment, review, or attention parallel. Fewer reserves one slot for the bet that deserves those scarce things, and one maintenance slot for obligations that cannot stop.

The app never auto-selects the winner. It surfaces evidence and makes the trade-off visible; the decision stays yours.
